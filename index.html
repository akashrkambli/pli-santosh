Important Disclosures

  • * Maturity proceeds from PLI Santosh Endowment Assurance, including sum assured and accrued bonuses, are fully tax-exempt under Section 10(10D) of the Income Tax Act, provided the annual premium does not exceed 10% of the sum assured.
  • * Bonus rates are subject to change as per Govt. Norms. Current rates are historical illustrations.
  • * Loans on PLI Santosh Endowment Assurance become available after 3 full years of policy premiums have been paid. This is against the policy's acquired surrender value, up to 90% of that value.
  • * Surrender value is calculated based on premiums paid, policy term, and surrender factors (typically 30% after 3-5 years, increasing thereafter).
  • * No bonus is paid if surrendered before 5 policy years; proportionate bonus on reduced sum assured applies after 5 years.

The Santosh Endowment Plan is a flagship product of India Post. All returns are backed by the Sovereign Guarantee of the Government of India. Returns on Investment sheets provided are illustrations based on current bonus rates.
